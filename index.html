About cafe

CoffeeShop is a small, unique coffee shop in Lviv. We offer a great variety of specialty coffee drinks as well as sandwiches, salads and soups. We also serve wine and beer and a variety of appetizers.

Do you know?..

Coffee was first banned in Mecca in 1511 because leaders believed it stimulated radical thinking. And, 16th century Italian clergymen tried to ban coffee because they believed it to be "satanic." However, Pope Clement VII loved coffee so much that he lifted the ban and had coffee baptized in 1600. But Ottoman leader Murad IV took it even further when he ascended the throne in 1623 by creating the first punishments for drinking coffee, which included beatings and being thrown into the sea.

Coffee is the second most popular beverage in the world – it’s second only to water. After crude oil, coffee is the most sought after commodity in the world. Caffeine improves your performance when working out by increasing adrenaline levels, and releasing fatty acids. It takes as little as 10 minutes for the caffeine in coffee to have an effect.
